WORKING PROCESS

First of all, please contact me anyhow (preferably through the contact form on this page). If You can provide a briefing about your needs, and I will respond with a concrete proposal with a closed budget. I always work with a closed budget, it's better for you, so there won't be any awful surprises at the end. I always try to adjust de budget to the maximum, and You will have a listing of all the work included (and also maybe some things not included).

When it's a design project, you will have to answer a little questionnaire that will help me know your style, preferences, etc. If you have some previous ideas, please let me know in advance, in my experience the results are much more satisfactory when I have that kind of feedback.

In any case, I will provide a set of three sketches (minimum) and with your feedback there will be a final sketch for finishing de work. The primary design will be done in black, white and grey gradients. We will choose the colour in another phase, where I will make three proposals again and We will find the final colours toghether.

I am very perfectionist, and I like to prioritize customer satisfaction above all. Therefore, I will not close the project until You are satisfied.

I am not a long experienced professional designer. But I have been amateur designing for 10 years, and my best asset is that I love doing it. I hope We can do business together.

Please, check my Social Media if You want. See You soon!
